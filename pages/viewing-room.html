Era Overview

[PLACEHOLDER TEXT] Chasing the Sun represents the current and only active world in the Hub. This era embodies 1980s-inspired aesthetics with neon-inflected visuals and intentional use of analog glitch textures. Light distortion, scan lines, and chromatic aberration are used deliberately to evoke themes of movement, yearning, artificial warmth, and repetition.

Visual Language

Neon pinks, electric blues, ultraviolet purples, and sunset oranges serve as accents and motion effects throughout this world. The neon and glitch aesthetic is contained within Chasing the Sun pages, era trailers, and era-specific transitions, preserving visual restraint across the rest of the Hub.
